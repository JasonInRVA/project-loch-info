What this document is

Minutes from the Chesterfield County Board of Supervisors meeting held on June 25, 2025.

Project relevance

The minutes record the board's formal approval of a fixed tax rate agreement with Project Loch, also identified as Aeris, and note that the agreement was the same as the one approved for Project Skye.

Why it matters

This closes the gap between the agenda packet and the agreement itself by documenting the official county action in the board's own meeting record.
