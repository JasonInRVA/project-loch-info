What this document is

This is DEQ's July 8, 2026 email asking the applicant to confirm whether it was withdrawing the SSWD for the originally proposed permittee-responsible mitigation site.

Why it matters

This short email translates the applicant's July 2 mitigation update into a direct administrative question. It is the clearest agency-side signal that DEQ understood the mitigation plan to have changed in a way that could require withdrawing the Wauford Property SSWD.

What the document says

DEQ confirms receipt of the applicant's materials and then asks whether, given the updated mitigation plan, the applicant is withdrawing the SSWD for the originally proposed PRM site. The agency says it needs that confirmation to update the matter administratively if required.

Why it belongs with the July 2 and July 8 applicant records

On its own, this email does not confirm a withdrawal. Its significance comes from reading it together with the July 2 response that abandoned PRM and the applicant's same-day confirmation that the SSWD should indeed be withdrawn.
