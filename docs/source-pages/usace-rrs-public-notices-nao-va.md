District, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers \\(Corps\\) received a complete prospectus on March 04, 2026, proposing the establishment of the Bobwhite Mitigation Bank to offset non-tidal wetland and stream The purpose of this notice is to solicit comments and recommendations from the…\nSPONSOR:   \nBridgeport Mitigation LLC\n2805 Park Avenue\nRichmond, Virginia 23221\nAttn: Mr. James Parker\nAGENT:         \nClearwater Ventures LLC\n2805 Park Avenue\nRichmond, Virginia 23221\nAttn: Mr. James Parker\nWATERWAY AND LOCATION: The proposed compensatory mitigation bank \\(the bank\\) is located at the terminus of Worsham Road in Powhatan, Virginia 23139. The bank’s approximate center coordinates are 37.480856, -77.914128. The bank drains to the Appomattox River. The bank was a managed loblolly pine plantation. The bank limits are approximately 108 acres.\nPROPOSED WORK and PURPOSE: The sponsor is proposing:\n- 61 acres of wetland rehabilitation,\n- 1,568 linear feet of stream restoration,\n- 7.4 acres of wetland enhancement,\n- 9 acres of wetland preservation,\n- 5.7 acres of riparian area enhancement,\n- 7.2 acres of upland buffer enhancement, and\n- 14.6 acres of upland buffer preservation.\nThe purpose of the bank is to provide off-site compensatory mitigation for projects that result in unavoidable impacts to wetlands and streams in the bank’s proposed service areas.\nThe bank’s proposed primary service area comprises the middle James River and includes hydrologic unit codes \\(HUCs\\) 02080207, 02080206; 02080205, and 02080203. The bank’s proposed secondary service area comprises HUCs 02080208, 02080204, and portions of HUCs 02080201 and 02080202.\nOversight of the project would be by an existing group of federal and state agency representatives. This interagency oversight group is known as the Interagency Review Team \\(IRT\\). The Corps and the Virginia Department of Environmental Quality \\(DEQ\\) chair the IRT.\nThe Corps, DEQ, and the Virginia Marine Resources Commission provide no guarantee that any individual or general permit will be granted authorization to use the project to compensate for unavoidable wetland and stream impacts associated with a permit verified, authorized, or issued by the Corps, pursuant to Section 404 of the Clean Water Act and Section 10 of the Rivers…\nA copy of the bank’s public notice and prospectus can be found on the Corps’ Regulatory In-lieu Fee and Bank Information Tracking System \\(RIBITS\\) website:\n[Public Notices \\(army.mil\\)](ribits.ops.usace.army.mil\/ords\/f)\n[https:\/\/ribits.ops.usace.army.mil\/ords\/f?p=107:278:4355772104855::::P278_BANK_ID:7355](ribits.ops.usace.army.mil\/ords\/f2)\nAUTHORITY: Issuance of public notices regarding proposed compensatory mitigation sites is required pursuant to Compensatory Mitigation for Losses of Aquatic Resources; Final Rule as published April 10, 2008, in the Federal Register, Vol. 73, No. 70, pages 19,594-19,705 \\(33 CFR 325 and 332 and 40 CFR 230\\).
