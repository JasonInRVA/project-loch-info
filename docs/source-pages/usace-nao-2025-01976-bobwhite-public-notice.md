## Notice Summary (Cleaned)

The Norfolk District states it received a complete prospectus on March 4, 2026, for the proposed Bobwhite Mitigation Bank. The proposed bank is intended to provide off-site compensatory mitigation for future authorized wetland and stream impacts.

The notice identifies the sponsor as Bridgeport Mitigation LLC and lists the bank location in Powhatan County. It describes proposed wetland and stream rehabilitation/enhancement/preservation work and identifies proposed primary/secondary service areas.

The notice states the project would be reviewed by an interagency team including USACE and DEQ and invites public comment by the listed deadline.
