I’ve put together a neutral, source-based guide to Google’s proposed Project Loch data center campus in Chesterfield County. It summarizes the project, links to the Army Corps and Virginia permit records, highlights changes that have occurred during review, and includes practical questions residents may want to ask at Google’s open house.

I’m sharing it as background information rather than as an argument for or against the project. The public record is complicated and still evolving, and I think everyone benefits from being able to see the original documents, understand what regulators have asked, and distinguish established facts from claims that are still being evaluated.
